Grammy Awards 2025

The Grammy Awards, often referred to simply as the Grammys, are annual awards presented by the Recording Academy to recognize outstanding achievement in the music industry. Established in 1959, the Grammys honor artists, producers, and songwriters across various genres and categories, including pop, rock, hip-hop, country, jazz, and classical music.
The awards are considered one of the most prestigious recognitions in the music industry, akin to the Oscars in film. Winners are selected through a voting process involving members of the Recording Academy, which consists of music professionals. The ceremony typically features live performances and is broadcasted on television, attracting a large audience each year.
